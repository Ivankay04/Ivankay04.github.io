About

The flagship
expedition

Katon-Karagay National Park is a must for anyone serious about Kazakhstan. Lake Markakol, Rakhmanovskie Springs, Radostny Pass, Lake Yazevoye — this is East Kazakhstan at its most spectacular.

The programme is fully flexible: choose your duration and accommodation — guesthouses or a tented camp. We add our own off-road detours to places the standard tours simply don't reach.

Programme

The best of
East Kazakhstan

Lake Markakol — one of Kazakhstan's largest mountain nature reserves, home to endemic fish (the uskuch) and rare migratory birds. Pristine and protected.

Rakhmanovskie Springs — radon thermal pools at 1,700 metres, surrounded by mountains. Radostny Pass and Lake Yazevoye — scenery you can't describe, only experience.

Our team adds exclusive off-road routes — locations that don't appear on any map and that most tour operators have never visited.
