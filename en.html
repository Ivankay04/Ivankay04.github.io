03 · Who we are

More than
a tour team

Asferia Adventure is a licensed tour operator based in Ridder, East Kazakhstan. Since 2023 we have been organising expeditions into the region's most remote and spectacular natural locations.

We offer end-to-end travel solutions for international guests: certified guides, fully equipped expedition vehicles, accommodation through trusted partners, airport transfers and full in-country support — a single booking, everything included.

Our cuisine on day tours features artisan lunch boxes in partnership with Krem café — warm, varied and always included in the price. Every detail is taken care of, so you can focus entirely on what's around you.
